About Us

Global Pilot Limited (GPL) is a Nigerian Oil and Gas Service Company headquartered in Lagos with an operational base in Port Harcourt.

GPL was incorporated in 2014 as a Subsea Service Company. In 2018 the company launched its Project Management, Drilling, and Integrated Well Intervention Service Lines with the aim of providing solutions to operational and production challenges faced by operators in the oil industry.

Knowledge, Teamwork, and Integrity are essential to service quality, and the company leverages these principles to deliver value to its Clients.

GPL targets both international and independent indigenous operators and offers services spanning land, swamp, and deep offshore environments.

Our Vision

To be known as a leader in Well Intervention Services, providing quality technical solutions that will bring productivity increase and profitability.

Our Mission

Value creation by using defined processes and industry leading technologies to deliver cost effective and high quality services in an efficient and safe manner.

Our Values

It is the core value of our business to provide our clients with innovative technical solutions that will meet and exceed our clients expectation. Our Integrity, Honesty and High Ethical Standard is the key to value delivery.
